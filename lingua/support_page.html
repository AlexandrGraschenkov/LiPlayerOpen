Frequently Asked Questions
How can I watch my favorite TV show in the app?
You have two options:
  • Use the built-in browser to find and watch the TV show online.
  • Upload the video file from your computer directly to your iPhone or iPad.
How does the app translate subtitles?
Lingua Player uses a combination of translation engines: Yandex, Google, DeepL, a built-in Offline AI, and even large language models (LLMs). You can choose the source that works best for you.
Can Lingua Player work without internet?
Yes. Once you've downloaded a video and subtitles, everything works offline. You can even translate subtitles using the custom offline AI while flying or with no connection at all.
What data does the app collect?
The app collects two types of anonymous data:
  • Feature usage statistics to understand user preferences and improve the product.
  • Crash logs including call stack traces to fix bugs.
No personal data is collected or shared. You can disable analytics in app settings if desired.
